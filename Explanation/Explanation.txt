Step 1. Words are tokenized and annotated with parts-of-speech tags.
Step 2. By using POS- tagging function(from NLTK) we categorize the words of sentence as Nouns and Verbs.
Step 3. Classifies nouns are added to the graph as vertices.
Step 4. An edge is added between nouns that co- occur.
Step 5. Using Word Overlap algorithm we extract the phrases and use them as relations between nouns.
Step 6. Using prediction model, we develop the relations between nouns.
Step 7. Fitted the model as required.
